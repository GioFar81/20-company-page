Statement

My name is Giovanni. I’m an Italian photographer based in East London. I’m in my third year of studies towards a bachelor in Photography at The School of Art, Architecture and Design, part of the London Metropolitan University.

I started my adventure years ago, shooting mainly cityscapes and architecture. During the journey, I shifted my focus towards portraiture, which is now my main focus. And currently working on my latest project, where I combine portraits with CGI.
